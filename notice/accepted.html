Your Moderator Journey Begins!

Welcome to the SDSRP Moderation Team. As a new Dizzy moderator, here's a guide to help you get started:

1. Review Moderation Guidelines

Familiarize yourself with the guidelines and policies. Be the guardian of our community standards.

2. Contact Dizzy Staff

Reach out to a Dizzy staff member for guidance on your new role. Ask about your next steps and how to contribute effectively.

3. Attend Orientation/Training

Participate in orientation to learn about moderation tools, protocols, and procedures.

4. Understand User Behavior Standards

Be knowledgeable about acceptable user behavior to enforce community standards fairly.

5. Familiarize with Moderation Tools

Learn to use the provided moderation tools to monitor and manage interactions and reported issues.
